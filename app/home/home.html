Fuel Economizer
About Fuel Economizer
Fuel Economizer is a currently in-development application to track fuel economy and maybe other things about your vehicle. Right now we're in active development (check it out on github) and registration is manual (contact me if you want to sign up).
Why Fuel Economy
How much do you spend on gas a year? How many miles do you drive in a month? What is the actual cost of maintaining your car? You probably don't know, really. And it's no surprise - it would take a lof of effort to gather all of that information and turn it into cool, useful graphs.

But fear no more! Fuel Economizer can provide you with all that and more - for almost no effort! Just open it up while you're at the pump, punch in three immediately-available data points (odometer reading, gas purchased, and total price paid) and be on your way. After a few entries, Fuel Economizer will begin displaying valuable information, whenever you're interested in looking.
